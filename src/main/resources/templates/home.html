BackUp Brain

A place where you schedule and track tasks that you need to do. Whether it's going grocery shopping, work tasks, events, or even giving your loved ones a call, BackUp Brain give you a space to store the things you need to remember and keep you on track.

Track Your Progress on your tasks for the day. Focus on today's task, so that you don't feel overwhelmed with the other tasks ahead of you.

Display the categorical composition of what all of your tasks consist of and determine how well you are balancing your work, social, and academic experiences.
